腾讯

腾讯抓到的API包含备案、WHOIS、http状态

  1. 备案状态查询
    请求地址: https://wss.cloud.tencent.com/dns/api/tool/domains_beian
    可用参数:domains
    提交方式:GET
    Ex:https://wss.cloud.tencent.com/dns/api/tool/domains_beian?domains=ourpast.cn
    返回数据类型:json
    Ex:{"status":{"code":0,"message":"\u64cd\u4f5c\u5df2\u7ecf\u6210\u529f\u5b8c\u6210","datetime":"2019-02-26 19:58:00"},"result":{"domains_status":{"ourpast.cn":{"tencent_beian_ok":false,"tencent_beian_ing":false,"gov_beian_ok":true,"gov_beianId":"\u9c81ICP\u590717042593\u53f7"}}}}

  2. 查看http状态
    请求地址: https://wss.cloud.tencent.com/dns/api/tool/http_curl_for_status
    可用参数:domain
    提交方式:GET
    Ex:https://wss.cloud.tencent.com/dns/api/tool/http_curl_for_status?domain=r0.gs
    返回数据类型:json
    Ex:{"status":{"code":0,"message":"\u64cd\u4f5c\u5df2\u7ecf\u6210\u529f\u5b8c\u6210","datetime":"2019-02-26 20:08:44"},"result":{"status_code":301}}

  3. whois查询
    请求地址: https://wss.cloud.tencent.com/buy/api/domains/domain/whois_info
    可用参数:domain
    提交方式:POST
    Ex:https://wss.cloud.tencent.com/buy/api/domains/domain/whois_info postdata{"domain":"r0.gs"}
