    请求地址: http://www.kuaidi100.com/autonumber/autoComNum
    可用参数:resultv2,text
    提交方式:GET
    Ex:http://www.kuaidi100.com/autonumber/autoComNum?resultv2=1&text=75121384132040
    返回数据类型:json
    Ex:{"comCode":"","num":"75121384132040","auto":[{"comCode":"zhongtong","id":"","noCount":3668,"noPre":"751213","startTime":""}]}
